DJ AK（DJ富贵DJ Fugui）

2018年于云南出道，中国夜场行业正处于巅峰转向下行的转折期。凭借对电子音乐的热爱与极强的学习能力，他从一名营销岗位的新人逐步成长为独当一面的DJ。因其涉猎广泛、技能全面，曾被同事称为"万能小王子"。Debuted in Yunnan in 2018, during the transition of China's nightclub industry from its peak to a downturn. With a passion for electronic music and exceptional learning ability, he grew from a marketing newcomer to a versatile DJ. Known among colleagues as the "All-Round Prince" due to his diverse skills.

现任职于老挝万象TG CLUB，负责包厢DJ部门业绩整理与财务对接工作。TG CLUB是老挝万象最具代表性的电音娱乐场所之一，以5D全屏科技和顶级音响设备著称。Currently working at TG CLUB in Vientiane, Laos, managing party room DJ department performance reports and finance coordination. TG CLUB is one of Vientiane's most iconic electronic music venues, known for its 5D full-screen technology and top-tier sound systems.
